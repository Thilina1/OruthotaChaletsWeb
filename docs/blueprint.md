# **App Name**: StayHaven

## Core Features:

- Reservation Details: Allow guests to view all their reservation information, including dates, room type, and total cost.
- Room Service Ordering: Enable guests to browse a digital menu, place orders, and specify delivery preferences.
- Amenity Exploration: Provide a comprehensive guide to all hotel amenities, including hours, location, and booking options. Display restaurant and bar information including generative AI descriptions and recommendations of popular choices based on guest history or other trending preferences.
- Staff Contact: Offer multiple channels for guests to contact hotel staff, such as live chat, request form, or direct call.
- Personalized Recommendations: Use generative AI as a tool to offer tailored recommendations for local attractions, dining, and activities based on guest preferences and history. 

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Warm Beige (#F5F5DC) to evoke a sense of comfort and hospitality.
- Background color: Off-White (#FAFAFA) for a clean and modern aesthetic, with very slight beige tinge.
- Accent color: Burnt Sienna (#E97451) for highlights and interactive elements.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for clear and accessible body text.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for elegant headlines; pair with 'PT Sans' for body.
- Use modern, minimalist icons for easy navigation. Represent key functions using iconography for universal understandability.
- Implement a card-based layout to clearly present each piece of information, utilizing negative space and clear section headings for effortless readability.
